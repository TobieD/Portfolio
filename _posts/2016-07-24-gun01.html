This was a school assignment to understand the workflow of creating a 3d game asset and understanding the way textures are made for physical based rendering.The model has an Albedo, Roughness, Metal and Normal map.

The workflow used was:

  • Model high poly
  • Model low poly
  • Normal map baking in 3ds max
  • Texturing Albedo, Metal, Roughness and Normal map
  • Import assets in Unreal Engine 4 and create material + light setup
