During my internship at Cartamundi Digital I got the opportunity to research Augmented Reality in Unity 3D using Vuforia. For a demo I had to come up with an interactive app that allowed the user to interact with an Augmented Environment/

I decided to have a dragon fly around some islands and the user could select the island the dragon would fly to by tapping on an island.
